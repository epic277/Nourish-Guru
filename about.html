About Suman Krishnaraj

Clinical Dietitian • Public Health Strategist • Metabolic Health Specialist

I’m Suman Krishnaraj, a Clinical Dietitian with a master’s degree in Clinical Dietetics & Applied Nutrition, a strong foundation in Microbiology & Chemistry, and 8+ years of experience shaping health outcomes for individuals, hospitals, FMCG brands, and global wellness companies.

My Journey

I began my career in a multispecialty hospital where I built the dietary department from scratch. Later, I worked with Herbalife, delivering nutrition training & product science education across India.

My next chapter took me into the world of FMCG, where I worked with Unilever and other giants, blending nutrition science, consumer insights, and product innovation.

People don’t fail diets.
Diets fail people.

Because they ignore metabolism, hormones, psychology, and lifestyle realities.

So I created Nourish Guru — a space to help people transform their metabolism, mindset, and long-term relationship with food.

My Philosophy

Real food.

No deprivation.

No extreme diets.

Sustainable fat loss.

Behavior-driven coaching.

Clinical science + Indian tradition.

I believe health is not built in 30-day challenges.
It’s built through thousands of small, consistent choices.

Who I Work With

Women with PCOS & thyroid imbalances

My Vision

To help India eat better, not through fear-based nutrition, but through science, simplicity, and culture-rooted food wisdom.
